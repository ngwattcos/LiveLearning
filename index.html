Live! Learning

Your Own Personalized Online Classroom

Live Learning provides a way for instructors to interact with their students through our website. They will be able to engage their students in real-time through an online classroom. It follows the same learning principles of Khan Academy - placing a blank canvas in front of the student to minimize distractions - with the benefits of live, social learning.
